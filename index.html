Campaign Funding and Prediction Markets

Quinn Herrera and David Deng

Introduction

From the Federal Election Commision(FEC) website, we downloaded the data of all individual contributions made to Presidential Candidates in the 2016 cycle. Relevant information include the amount of contribution, the date of contribution, and the origin of contribution.

We also scraped prediction market data from the PredictWise website, in which time series data on candidate's expected probability to win in each state's primary and overall nomation is given.
