Introduction

From the Federal Election Commision(FEC) website, we downloaded the data of all individual contributions made to Presidential Candidates in the 2016 cycle. Relevant information include the amount of contribution, the date of contribution, and the origin of contribution.

We first looked at the expenditure pattern in President Obama's 2012 reelection campaign.
